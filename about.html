Chief Executive Officer & Design Director

Executive Profile

Jafar Sadhiq leads Whimpro at the intersection of high-end architectural design and precision manufacturing.

Jafar Sadhiq E. is an Multidisciplinary Designer, Interior Architect,entrepreneur, and the visionary Founder and CEO of Whimpro (Whimsical Project LLP). Driven by a philosophy that balances structural precision with artistic expression, Jafar has built an end-to-end design ecosystem that redefines modern living and commercial spaces. Under his leadership, Whimpro has evolved into a premier multidisciplinary firm renowned for its high-end, minimalist aesthetic and seamless execution.

With a deep commitment to architectural excellence, advanced manufacturing, and technological innovation, Jafar bridges the gap between grand architectural concepts and meticulous reality. He oversees a diverse portfolio that seamlessly integrates comprehensive architectural services, high-precision modular manufacturing, and custom luxury product lines.
